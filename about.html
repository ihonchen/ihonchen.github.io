Hello! I'm I-hon (Henry) Chen, a senior at University of California, San Diego. I major in cognitive science specializing in human computer interaction with a minor in business, specifically product marketing + management. Studying human computer interaction allows me to incorporate technical theories into my design process, which focuses on questioning everything to make strategic and immersive designs. Also, having a minor in business allows me to understand both user and market research, as well as how to effectively manage products and projects. In my free time I enjoy traveling, and after spending half a year studying abroad in Singapore and more just roaming around in other parts of Asia (Malaysia, China, Taiwan, Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia, Japan), I have broadened my international perspective and honed my photography skills. I'd love to talk to you about anything from why Kirby is the best video game character of all time to where to get the best Vietnamese coffee in Saigon! Let's connect!
